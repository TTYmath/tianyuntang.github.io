About Me

I am currently a research fellow at Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA) in National University of Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2024 from the Department of Mathematics, National University of Singapore under the supervision of Prof. Kim-Chuan Toh. Back in 2020, I received my B.S. degree from the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Science and Technology of China.
